About Me

I am future faculty fellow postdoc at University of Illinois Urbana Champaign department of Computer Science supervised by Dakshita Khurana and Makrand Sinha. My research interests are broadly quantum complexity theory and quantum information theory. In particular, I use tools from various mathematical disciplines such as high dimensional probability theory and representation theory to understand how quantum mechanics plays role in computational and communication problems. Before joining UIUC, I hold two postdoc positions at Tufts University and University of Amsterdam. I also completed my PhD at Georgia Tech under supervision of Matthieu Bloch.
